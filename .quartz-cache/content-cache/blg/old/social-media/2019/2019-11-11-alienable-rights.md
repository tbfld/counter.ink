In the future, human rights will be 💯 alienable because they’ll be transactional: you’ll get exactly and only what you give, and what you give will be the sum total of everything you’ve ever done, said, and even felt. It’ll be everything guys like this dreamed of, except for one thing: they’ll fare worst of all.
