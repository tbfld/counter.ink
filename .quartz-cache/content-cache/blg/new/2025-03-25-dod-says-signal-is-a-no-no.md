The plot thickens. [NPR reports](https://www.npr.org/2025/03/25/nx-s1-5339801/pentagon-email-signal-vulnerability) this image was taken from a Pentagon-wide email circulated on 18 March. The headline says “Ahead of the Signal leak,” which is true and not: Goldberg was added on the 11th, the thread took place on the 13th, and the attacks were on the 15th — so this warning came three days later than the events, but six days before Goldberg's story ran. That means it came *after* he started asking questions, which to my ear suggests this may well have been DoD bureaucracy (a) doing the right thing opsec-wise AND ALSO — because this kind of signaling and maneuvering is how bureaucracies work — moving to undermine the people involved in the chat.
