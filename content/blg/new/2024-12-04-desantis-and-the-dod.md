If the rumors about DeSantis for Sec Def turns out to be serious, the media flap will focus on his “anti-woke” noise and jokes about his white boots etc, but ignore all that. He’s an *extremely* calculating authoritarian from a state where public corruption is a religion, and as governor he has systematically worked to empower armed forces and hobble civil rights. There’s a name for that mix, and you can guess what it is.

Normally I’d ignore the rumors because Trump’s hobby is teasing and humiliating DeSantis (and part of me thinks DeSantis “doesn’t come here for the hunting,” as that filthy old joke goes), but that *could* take the form of elevating him to Sec Def only to fire him at some point.

As I’ve reminded people every time the specter of DeSantis rises, he’s *desperate*: facing term limits as governor, no Senate spots opening up (maybe? see below), the House would be a step down his ultra-ambitious wife would never tolerate, he’s radioactive for education (though maybe less so for a big law job now?), and a think-tank gig would be a road to nowhere). So he’s extra-extra-desperate, which means he’ll agree in advance to do anything and everything Trump demands. And I mean *anything and everything*.
