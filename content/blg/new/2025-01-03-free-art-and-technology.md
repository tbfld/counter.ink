[Leonardo is looking for an editor-in-chief.](https://leonardo.info/opportunity/leonardo-book-series-editor-in-chief) What a great job! Oh, wait, it isn’t exactly a *job*, it’s a “volunteer position”: no pay, no bennies, no mention of reimbursements. A fair number of people at ASU, MIT, and Leonardo  signed off on advertising a role that could *easily* be full-time, but somehow failed to convince their respective educational institutions — two of which are rolling in dough — that whoever they “hire” deserves a living salary at the very least.
