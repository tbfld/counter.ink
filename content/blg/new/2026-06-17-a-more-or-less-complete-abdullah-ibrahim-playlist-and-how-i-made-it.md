More and more I’ve found myself wanting complete-ish chronological playlist of this or that artist — most recently, for Abdullah Ibrahim, who passed away a few days ago. (Sean Jacobs has written a beautiful piece about him in NYRB — link below.) But those playlists are often hard to come by, so I thought I’d try my hand at some vibe-coding. Result:

1. a pretty complete [chronological discography for Ibrahim on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/092Y1pjjk2S09aLUIlB07t?si=0a86c6290da64e0c)
2. the [same thing in JSON format](https://github.com/tbfld/abdullah-ibrahim-chronological-discography)
3. a basic toolkit in Python for [turning *any* discography in JSON format into a Spotify playlist](https://github.com/tbfld/playlist-gen)

Sean’s piece on Ibrahim, [which I really recommend.](https: //www.nybooks.com/online/2026/06/07/songs-of-liberation-abdullah-ibrahim/) (‘archived’ version [here](http://archive.today/2026.06.11-142621/https://www.nybooks.com/online/2026/06/07/songs-of-liberation-abdullah-ibrahim/)).

Share and enjoy!

**Note:** I’m not a Spotify fan *at all*, but all the alternatives I've tried seem fatally flawed.
