Everyone seems surprised that a handful of mega corps are vulnerable to political control, as if the left hadn’t been warning about that for decades. But rather than look backward, let’s look forward and ask what *is* resistant to political control? If it isn’t journalism, big tech, law firms, universities, or media, what is it? Easy: people. So rather than ritually reenacting this faux betrayal each time some sector shows its true colors, try asking yourself questions like: Where do you draw the line? What are you willing to risk? What will you demand from “opposition” politicians? How will you protect everyone who depends on you?
