1. conjured up an extralegal magical entity, DOGE, that was going to cut some ridiculous percentage of the federal civil service,
2. from Day One terrorized employees with a “shock and awe” blizzard of illegal, incoherent, and inconsistent Executive Orders,
3. followed up with overheated rhetoric announcing witch hunts and firings,
4. reclassified a huge number of them in a way that strips them of most labor protections,

There are several more details that would fit in neatly, but it’s the storyline that matters.
