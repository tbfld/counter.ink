Isn’t this [“hyperdemocracy” noise](https://www.dissentmagazine.org/online_articles/andrew-sullivan-trump-concern-trolling-for-democracy) mostly a nostalgic revisitation and extension of Trilateral rhetoric about an “excess of democracy”? (Not the imaginary conspiracy, I mean the book, as in ISBN 978-0814713655). A lot of that 1970s folksy authentism — not the disco / big hair / PBR that clever people keep rediscovering, I’m thinking of the permanent bicentennial ice cream parlor “shoppe” stuff, which keeps being forgotten — was a nostalgic revisitation and extension of an imaginary 1950s. What was so great abou— *oh, right,* that was when the federal government was giving lots of money to white guys to go to school, get jobs, buy houses, etc.
