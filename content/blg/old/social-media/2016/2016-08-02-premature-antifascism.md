Come on, people, time’s running out to be an official Premature Antifascist! It’s easy — all you need to do is write a comment saying something like “the GOP has become a fascist party.” And it’s safe too: today’s NYT has an editorial illustrated with a picture that equates Trump with Hitler. If the NYT’s editorial board can do it, surely you can do it too! (And if you can’t quite bring yourself to do it, ask yourself: what exactly would need to happen before you could say it?)
