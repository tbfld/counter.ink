This photo of Zuck doing some kind of smurfy thing (this is a crop) will get lots of larfs, but what jumps out is his mime-like sunscreen mask. For all anyone knows, his family may have a history of skin cancer; if so, maybe a bit more on his ears and neck would be good? It’s hard to tell from the photo, and facial skin has peculiar properties. But my guess is that his face is slathered because his pallor is part of his ‘brand’ as a geek and somehow still young. A deep tan would dilute that by suggesting he’s an aging robber baron. So the photo, no, not so interesting; but the window it opens up into how he’s constructed is: his stumbling and relentless reliance on tired, empty phrases might be forgivable in a young man, but not so much for someone with his responsibilities.
