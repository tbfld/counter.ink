A ***brilliant*** take on FB pocketing a few of my posts/comments by \[BB\: “You are today the third person who posts screenshot of their censored post. This tells me two things in addition to the scale of something being changed in the background in the filtering infrastructure. First, that they don’t filter the screenshots of filtered posts, for either technical reasons or political ones. Second, them allowing the circulation of the content, with the proof that they censored it is itself a form of registering their objection, but not blocking actual circulation: a way out from their filtering conundrum.”
