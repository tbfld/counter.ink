“Inside the Barnes Center at the Arch, the university’s new $50 million student wellness center, hundreds of students have splayed themselves on the floor... They have been clamoring for the university to meet a long list of demands, including the expulsion of those involved in the hate speech and more systemic changes like diversifying the faculty and reforming the curriculum to educate students on racism.” 👉🏼 You gotta wonder how much “wellness” that $50M could’ve brought about if it wasn’t spent on white-elephant architecture. Syracuse’s undergrad cost of attendance is $75K, so assuming students get 50% FA that’d give 333 of them a four-year free ride. Like, starting off post-grad life *debt-free*. Yeah, I know (almost certainly in much more detail than you) how many intertwingled pros and cons there are. But the irony of students camping out in a facility that trumpets their wellness while miring them in debt... And that’s before we even get to what they’re protesting about.
