I’m coming around to the opinion, which isn’t as paradoxical as it sounds, that opinions are rubbish. All of them, they’re a crap form of human thought. No doubt there’s some Wildean bon mot waiting to be birthed there — be my guest. The main thing is the gist. I expect we’ll start hearing a lot of people saying things like this in a few years.
