Apparently there’s a whole series of big moons coming up, and I saw my first claim that so-and-so Native American tribe called this a “\[such-and-such] moon.” The details don’t matter, but internet-era fantasies — of made-up “wolf moons,“ “strawberry moons,” etc — being legitimized as *heep-um authentic*, that’s a predictable devolution. We should start calling them “router moons,” “wifi moons,” “document moons,” because that’s a lot closer to the truth. \#suburbanlegend
