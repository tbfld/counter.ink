I just realized that if MAGA flourishes, the question of how they order their epithets will become one of those bourdieuvian or however you spell that distinctions. Those with more attainment will tend to formalize it: “Now now little Ivankula, how many times do I need to tell you, attitude first and institution last! So it’s *woke radical left communist*, not *radical left woke communist*.” Those with lower attainment will face the constant challenge of ordering them bigly wrong and giving away away their humble origins. There might even be a mavericky school of thought — let’s call them Vanceniks — who deliberately modulate how they (mis)order epithets as a strategy for social mobility.
