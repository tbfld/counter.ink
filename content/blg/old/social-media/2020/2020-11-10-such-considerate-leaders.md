Who would have thought so many high-level officials would be so caring? Imagine, *resigning* at the height of your public career to help Trump through this difficult time. The head of the National Nuclear Security Administration, the chief of the DOJ’s election crimes division, the DoD’s head of policy. True friends. And to think of the others who no doubt would have followed suit, if only they’d had better models in their own times of need — firing them was the right thing to do. The Secretary of Defense, the NSA’s general counsel, the Exec Director responsible for the National Climate Assessment... We all need to share these examples and emphasize narratives about how what’s happening is *really* about Trump’s feelings.
