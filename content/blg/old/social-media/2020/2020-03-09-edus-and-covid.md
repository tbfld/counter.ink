Actual WHOA! One by one, edus are imposing remote learning in response to C-19. I think most admins were just trying to make it through midterms. [But Amherst just jumped way out in front:](https://www.amherst.edu/news/covid-19/messages-for-the-college-community/node/766550) they told students to *move out* by the end of the week because “the risk of having hundreds of people return from their travels to the campus is too great.” And they’re right, because dorms will turn out to be an even bigger issue than classes.
