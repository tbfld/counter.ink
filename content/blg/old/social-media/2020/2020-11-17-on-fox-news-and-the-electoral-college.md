Fox gave up on showing the EV count today; the only major outlet still doing it is Bloomberg, and it’s drifting toward the bottom of their page. The legal challenges are dead, the GA count is a dud, the man-child-snowflake stories are over, the insider stories about who’s nudging Trump this way or that came to an end, the fundraising scam stories ran out, the Trump News Network noise died down, and even the purge stuff finished. Even obstructing the transition is getting a bit long in the tooth. As he loses control of bureaucracies that no longer fear him, he’ll turn to things where executive action needs less and less cooperation — say, launching military attacks. It looks like the DoD decided to nip that in the bud by leaking the Iran story. But the guy still has two and a half months in office. So: what’s next?
