[This article](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/07/24/opinion/walmart-coronavirus-masks.html) presents national chains’ adoption of mask rules as something new, but it was very clear early on that they played *the* decisive role in establishing new hygienic norms on a national scale. Basically, if Home Depot and Costco were doing it, smaller retailers had to follow suit. Just as Amazon’s website has become the de-facto card catalog for the masses, big-box stores have become a form of media, or maybe even an arm of ‘the’ media.
