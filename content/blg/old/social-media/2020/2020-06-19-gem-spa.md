Like much of NYC, Gem Spa was passively crappy but you took it for granted; [now that it’s gone](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-8440235/Longtime-NYC-shop-Gem-Spa-frequented-Lou-Reed-Madonna-closes-pandemic.html
) it’ll be replaced by something actively awful. “Nostalgia” isn’t quite the right word for this feeling of counter-missing something you dislike less than whatever took its place. And, JFC, do we need a word for *that* these days.
