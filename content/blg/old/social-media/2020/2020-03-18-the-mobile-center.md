[Nice thread to start the day, America.](https://twitter.com/michaelharriot/status/1240129471165980676) More generally: in just a few weeks, we’ve watched as the political <LOLZ>center</LOLZ> has lunged from moderate Dems clutching at the pearls that Sanders is a SOCIALIST to Trump debating whether every American should get $1K or $2K per month. You can snark at this hypocrisy if you like, but more than anything it shows how fantastically mobile and flexible the ‘center’ is. And that’s a good thing. We’ll all have lots of opportunity to connect with everyone in our address books, and this is a good time to emphasize how important it is that *everyone* can look to government as a source of sustenance and hope in hard times.
