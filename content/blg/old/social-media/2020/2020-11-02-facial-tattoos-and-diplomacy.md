[This is the kind of moment](https://edition.cnn.com/2020/11/02/asia/new-zealand-foreign-minister-intl-hnk/index.html) we’ve seen mainly in sci-fi so far, where body modifications serve as proxies for, let’s say, something between a ‘planet’ and a ‘race.’ I don’t think there’s any question that Mahuta’s facial tattoos will unsettle a lot of other diplomats, and in doing so reveal how much the milieu of international relations remains a colonialist project.
