I’m *super* stoked to be working with one of the main instigators [of this site](https://moruroa-files.org/en/) on, as they say, his next-level project. That one weaves together a half-dozen woolly ideas that have been tugging at me for years, in some cases for decades, so it’s supremely satisfying to have a concrete context to articulate them — and for a project that, most of all, will benefit others. It’s also just GOOD in respects. Really, do yourself a favor and take the time to scroll through this site. You’ll be glad you did.
