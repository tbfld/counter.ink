I really enjoy [speculative discussions about aliens’ motives](https://www.washingtonpost.com/outlook/ufo-report-aliens-seti/2021/06/09/1402f6a8-c899-11eb-81b1-34796c7393af_story.html) — it’s like listening to two pineapples talk about Ingeborg Bachmann or something. This is especially good because it naively suggests an ambitious global regime for regulating attempts to communicate with aliens. I don’t think people have [grokked](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grok) the fact that we now live in a backwards age where prohibiting things is the best way to ensure they happen.
