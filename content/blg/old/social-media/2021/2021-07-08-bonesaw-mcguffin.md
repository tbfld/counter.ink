Pretty much the entirety of US media was morbidly fascinated with [that detail](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-9768803/Trump-told-Mike-Pompeo-Ive-never-bone-saw-Saudis-dismembered-Jamal-Khashoggi.html), so singling Trump out for it would be unfair (unless you think he should be held up as a moral exemplar — in which case you might want to game that out). But his witty repartee — "I've been in difficult negotiations, but I've never had to take a bone saw. Have you ever had to take a bone saw into negotiations?" — says a lot about why that detail was so ubiquitous: it served as a misdirection that made the monstrosity seem more palpable by making it *cinematic*. If that effect wasn't the intent behind leaking it, it *will* be for similar events in the future. In the meantime, Trump and his coterie deserve every single horror they'd so casually impose on others.
