Pretty much everyone knows that Stacey Abrams is the hero of this electoral cycle. Her vision and fortitude played a, probably *the* decisive role in delivering the White House and probably the Senate to the Dems. Less noted is the fact that these wins are a complete repudiation of the Clintonite f\*ck-’em non-approach of ceding entire swaths of the US to the GOP. It’s time to purge the Clinton- and Obama-era leadership of the DNC, DCCC, and DSCC. Abrams has proven that she can turn the tide on the Southern Strategy and (IMO) engineer the beginnings of a new realignment. The question the Dems should be asking is what more she can do. And any “centrists” who obstruct that should be driven out.
