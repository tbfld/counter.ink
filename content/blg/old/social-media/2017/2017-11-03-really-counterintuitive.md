[Perry’s remark](http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-5044499/Energy-chief-Perry-Fossil-fuels-prevent-sexual-assault.html) is just bog-standard pop technosocial history: pick a commodity then attribute unintended and ‘counterintuitive’ social effects to it. The key is the word ‘really,’ which is why this style appeals to Perry: he gets to say “You think teh oilz are bad but REALLY they’re good for the ladies!!!” This style of thought is endemic to an intellectual world consumed by externalities. Really.
