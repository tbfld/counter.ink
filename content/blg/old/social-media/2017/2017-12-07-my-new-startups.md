#### 2017-12-08

I’m delighted to unveil my new startup, which has been in stealth mode for the last few minutes: CoiCoin (COI), a proof-of-work cryptocurrency based on Conflicts of Interest. If you can prove you have a COI, I’ll give you a COIn. Cut it into funny shapes and, if someone you know has a COI then give them a shape; they do the same, ad aetheream. Since knives aren’t perfectly sharp, the shapes will tend to flatten out each time they’re cut, and eventually / theoretically all the COIns will have a greater surface area than the entire universe.
