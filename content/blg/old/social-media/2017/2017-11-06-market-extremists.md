Yesterday I asked if there was a better name for “libertarians.” The best answer was “market extremists.” Seriously, it’s time to stop dignifying that kind of aggressive political stance with any mention of liberty. Making lots of money is fine, whatever. But, beyond that, the power to impoverish, desolate, maim, and kill isn’t freedom, it’s the denial of others’ freedom. Try it: from now on just say “market extremist.” Tell your friends.
