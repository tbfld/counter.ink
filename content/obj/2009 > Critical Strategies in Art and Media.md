This [panel discussion](https://acfny.org/event/symposium-conference-critical-strategies-in-art-and-media/) (subtitled “Perspectives of New Cultural Practices” took place on 10 Sept 2009 at the Austrian Cultural Forum NY, with Steve Kurtz (Critical Art Ensemble), Amanda McDonald-Crowley ([Eyebeam](https://eyebeam.org/)), [Claire Pentecost](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claire_Pentecost) ([Continental Drift](https://abstractdecors.wordpress.com/maps-drifts/continental-drift/)), and [Peter Lamborn Wilson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Lamborn_Wilson) (independent), moderated by Konrad Becker ([World-Information Institute](http://world-information.org/wii/en)) and Jim Fleming ([Autonomedia](https://autonomedia.org/))
