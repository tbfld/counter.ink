The roving_reporter “[nym](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=Nym)” began as a way to anonymously contribute news items to the [nettime-l mailing list](https://www.nettime.org/). From there, it spawned [over seven hundred other nyms](https://www.nettime.org/Lists-Archives/nettime-l-1501/msg00041.html) on the list, and off the list it became [an occasional column](https://www.tbtf.com/roving_reporter/) on Keith Dawson’s [TBTF](https://tbtf.com/), where it  focused on the increasingly ridiculous antics of [ICANN](https://www.icann.org/), the California public-benefit corporation that aspired to become a global regulatory agency. It’s worth noting that the group of people Keith assembled as a sort of a brain trust around TBTF, the “[Irregulars](https://www.tbtf.com/the-irregulars.html),” is one of the greatest online communities I’ve ever had the honor to be a part of.
